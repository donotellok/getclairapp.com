Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Clair is currently in pre-launch. This page explains how we handle information submitted through the website waitlist form.

When you submit the waitlist form, we collect your email address so we can notify you about Clair launch updates.

How we use your information

We only use your email address to send launch-related updates for Clair. We do not sell your personal information.

Website form submissions are processed through Web3Forms. Their service may store and process your submission data according to their terms and privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain waitlist submissions for as long as needed to communicate launch updates or until you request deletion.

Your choices

You can request that we delete your information by contacting us at hello@getclairapp.com.

Contact
